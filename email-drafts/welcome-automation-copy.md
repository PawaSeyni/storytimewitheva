## Pre-flight checklist before activation

Before clicking **Enable automation**, verify each email has:

- [ ] Correct subject + preview text (per above)
- [ ] Hero image that fits the email's topic
- [ ] At least one CTA button linking to the correct PDF (live URLs verified)
- [ ] Greeting uses `{$name|default:"there"}` (or `amigo/a` / `à toi` for ES/FR fallbacks)
- [ ] Sign-off "Happy reading, Eva"
- [ ] Unsubscribe block at the bottom (MailerLite adds this by default)

Then: **Enable automation** in top right.

After enabling, you can use the **Test automation** button in the dashboard to send all 4 emails to your own address with no delays — sanity-check before real subscribers hit them.
