🃏 Eva's Matching Adventure

Find the matching pairs — flip two cards to see if they match!

✨ How to Play ✨

  1. Pick a theme: Animals, Colors, or Story Friends!
  2. Tap any card to flip it over
  3. Tap another card — if they match, they stay flipped! 🎉
  4. If they don't match, they'll flip back — try to remember!
  5. Find all the pairs to win! 🏆
