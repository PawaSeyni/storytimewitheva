🌍 Multilingual Word Flashcards

Tap a card to flip it and learn the word in another language!

✨ How to Play ✨

  1. Pick a category below (Colors, Animals, Numbers, or Feelings)
  2. Choose your language pair: English ↔ Spanish or English ↔ French
  3. Tap a card to flip it and see the translation!
  4. Say the word out loud in both languages
  5. Complete all cards to earn your Star Badge! ⭐
